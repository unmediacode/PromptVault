Privacy Policy

PromptVault for macOS

Last updated: February 15, 2026

PromptVault does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your prompts stay on your device.

Overview

PromptVault is a native macOS application for managing AI prompts locally on your computer. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app — it works entirely offline with no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information — We do not ask for your name, email, or any identifying information.
  • No analytics — We do not use any analytics or tracking frameworks.
  • No network requests — The app makes zero network connections. It works entirely offline.
  • No crash reporting — We do not collect crash logs or diagnostic data.
  • No third-party SDKs — The app is built exclusively with Apple frameworks.

Data Storage

All data you create in PromptVault (prompts, categories, preferences) is stored locally on your Mac using Apple's CoreData framework. This data:

  • Resides only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Is fully under your control — you can delete it at any time by removing the app

Clipboard Access

PromptVault accesses the system clipboard only when you explicitly choose to copy a prompt (via the Copy button or the keyboard shortcut). The app never reads from the clipboard — it only writes to it when you request it.

App Sandbox

PromptVault runs inside Apple's App Sandbox, which means it has no access to your files, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other system resources beyond what is strictly necessary to function.

Children's Privacy

PromptVault does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the changes on this page and update the "Last updated" date above. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
