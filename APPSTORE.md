## Description — English

```
PromptVault is a native macOS app to save, organize, search, and reuse your AI prompts.

Stop losing your best prompts in chat history. PromptVault gives you a dedicated space to build your personal prompt library — organized by categories, searchable instantly, and always one shortcut away from your clipboard.

KEY FEATURES

- Three-column layout — Browse categories, scan your prompt list, and edit in a spacious detail panel, all at once.
- Smart filters — Quickly access All prompts, Favorites, or Recent (last 7 days) with a single click.
- Categories with icons — Organize prompts into custom categories, each with its own SF Symbol icon.
- Instant search — Find any prompt in real time by title or content.
- Auto-save — Your edits are saved automatically as you type. No save button needed.
- One-click copy — Copy any prompt to the clipboard with a single click or ⌘⇧C. A "Copied!" confirmation lets you know it worked.
- Keyboard-first — ⌘N to create, ⌘F to search, ⌘⇧C to copy, ⌘⌫ to delete.
- Context menus — Right-click any prompt to copy, duplicate, move, favorite, or delete.
- Fully offline — No account, no cloud, no tracking. All your data stays on your Mac.
- Dark mode — Looks great in both Light and Dark mode, adapting automatically.

BUILT FOR MAC

PromptVault is a true native Mac app built with SwiftUI and CoreData — no Electron, no web views. It feels fast, familiar, and right at home on your Mac.

PERFECT FOR

- Developers who use AI coding assistants daily
- Writers crafting prompts for content generation
- Researchers building systematic prompt collections
- Anyone who wants to stop rewriting the same prompts
```
